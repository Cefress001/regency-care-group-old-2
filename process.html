How Our Process Works

A clear four‑step approach to match your family with a trusted caregiver.

Our Four‑Step Process

1

Discovery Call

We learn about your family's needs, schedule, personality preferences and budget range.

  • Needs and schedule discussion
  • Personality and lifestyle preferences
  • Budget range and employer obligations
2

Screening & Vetting

Our team conducts thorough background checks, reference verification and experience review.

  • Identity and background checks
  • Reference checks and interviews
  • Experience and training verification
  • Professionalism and reliability assessment
3

Curated Introductions

We present 2–3 vetted candidates and coordinate introductions or trial shifts.

  • Shortlist of 2–3 candidates with profiles
  • Interview and trial shift coordination
  • Guidance on questions and evaluation
  • Assistance finalising your choice
4

Confident Start & Ongoing Support

We remain available after placement for check‑ins, adjustments and guidance.

  • Start‑day coordination
  • Regular check‑ins with family and caregiver
  • Support and adjustments if issues arise
  • 90‑day replacement guarantee
