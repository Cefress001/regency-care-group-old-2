Support Tiers

Curated Placement

Best for families who want an exceptional caregiver match handled carefully and discreetly.

  • Discovery call and needs clarification
  • Curated shortlist (2–3 candidates)
  • Background and reference checks
  • Introductions coordination
  • Start‑day support

Placement + Continuity Support

Best for families who want stability after placement — not just a handoff.

  • Everything in Curated Placement
  • Post‑placement check‑ins
  • Adjustment support if issues arise
  • Replacement coordination within 90‑day guarantee

Ongoing Family Support

Best for families seeking a long‑term partnership with proactive care guidance.

  • Everything in Placement + Continuity Support
  • Monthly care reviews
  • Proactive caregiver support and coaching
  • Scheduling and transition assistance
  • Priority response
