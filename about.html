About Regency Care Group

Learn what makes us different and why families trust our private referral service.

Our Purpose

Regency Care Group was founded to solve a recurring problem in home care: families cycling through caregivers due to mismatches and miscommunication. We believe caregiving is a relationship, not a transaction. That’s why we listen deeply, understand your needs and values, and match you with a caregiver who feels like family.

Our mission is to provide Orange County families with safety, ease, value and ongoing support so you can trust that your loved one is in good hands.

Our Story

Our founder has spent decades in family services and coaching. Through this work she saw first‑hand how the right match can transform a household. Determined to raise the standard of private in‑home care, she built Regency to combine high‑touch service with careful screening and thoughtful matching.

Today, we serve families throughout Coastal Orange County—Newport Coast, Pelican Hill and beyond—earning referrals from estate managers, family offices and professionals who trust our commitment to discretion and excellence.

Our Values

Stability

We cultivate long‑term relationships and provide a stable care experience for families and caregivers alike.

Discretion

We handle every interaction with complete confidentiality and respect for your family's privacy.

Stewardship

We steward your trust by acting with integrity, transparency and care in every step of the process.

Partnership

We see ourselves as partners, not vendors. Our success is measured in enduring relationships and family harmony.

What Makes Us Different

We are not a home care agency — we refer vetted independent caregivers and guide you every step of the way. Families hire and supervise their caregivers directly, while we handle vetting, matching and ongoing support. Our approach blends the flexibility of private hiring with the safety net of a professional advisory service.
